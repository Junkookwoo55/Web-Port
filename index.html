about me

Junfeng Wu, an aspiring Interactive Game Designer based in Oakland, California, is passionate about crafting narrative-focused video games that offer immersive experiences. His early encounters with video games laid the foundation for his growing fascination with the game creation process. Junfeng's journey began with a childhood filled with gaming, fueling his ambition to develop a Triple-A level game that captivates audiences globally. Set to graduate in Fall 2023 with a B.F.A. in Interactive Game Design from California State University East Bay, Junfeng has already explored various facets of game development. His expertise extends to 3D modeling, sound production, storytelling, scriptwriting, and coding. Junfeng is continually seeking to broaden his skills, particularly in industry-standard software like Adobe, Autodesk Maya, Unity, and Unreal Engine. His goal is not just to create games, but to build worlds that resonate and engage players from all walks of life.
